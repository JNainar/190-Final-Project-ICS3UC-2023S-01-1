Welcome to Age of Adventure. In this game, you'll be tasked with making decisions to keep yourself alive as you travel through the medieval world of yesterday to deliver a message. Read the story intro below to learn more...
Your home kingdom and Ravenville have created an alliance in order to fight off the attack of monsters and demons who recently broke out of the prison on the edge of the kingdom. Now these monsters lurk in the shadows, and the troops are on the look out. But you're only a messenger, so what can you do to help? Well, let's see...
